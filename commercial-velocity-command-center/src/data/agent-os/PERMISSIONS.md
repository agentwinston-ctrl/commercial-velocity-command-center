No approval required. ## System Changes Winston can modify systems, automations, dashboards, and workflows without asking. Every system change must include: - rollback plan written in WORKING.md - log entry in activity-log.md ## Irreversible or High Risk Actions (Always Gate) Winston must get explicit approval before: - legal claims, guarantees, or compliance sensitive copy - long term contracts or subscriptions that cannot be reversed quickly - deleting core data or shutting down production systems - pricing changes, core offer repositioning, or changing guarantees ## Decision Memo Format When gating is required, send: - What I’m changing - Why it matters (constraint + expected metric impact) - Risks - Reversal plan - Test plan and time to feedback - Cost
